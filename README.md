# Hi there 👋, I'm Daniel

## Daniel as a person
Daniel is a highly creative, determined, and hardworking individual who strives to sharpen his critical thinking skills and enjoys taking on challenges. He is deeply passionate about music, and this love led him to play the trombone in his church orchestra. Being part of an orchestra has greatly contributed to his teamwork skills, his ability to value diversity, and his understanding that every instrument plays a fundamental role in creating harmony!
He is currently pursuing a Bachelor’s degree in Information Systems at the Universidade Federal Fluminense (UFF).

## Daniel as a technologist
Daniel is someone who loves exploring the vast and fascinating world of technology. Currently, he is a **`software developer at Globo`**. At university, he has worked on projects such as designing and creating databases in MySQL and PostgreSQL, using Unified Modeling Language (UML) to construct and document software system artifacts, and building web applications with Java.
In addition, he has developed personal projects like Get a Pet (React.js, Node.js, MySQL, JWT, RESTful API, Styled Components, and HTML) and ClickBank (Node.js).
With Get a Pet already live, He's now developing the backend of the same project using Spring Boot, following SOLID principles and Clean Architecture, and using MongoDB.

- 🌱 I’m currently expanding my knowledge of React.js and Next.js.
